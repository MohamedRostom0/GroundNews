The far-right Alternative for Germany party (AfD), projected to win second place in the upcoming elections, has rejected criticism on the use of the word biodeutsch ('organically German'), chosen by German linguists as the most malicious word of the year
In Germany, every year, a jury made up of at least five linguists and two guest judges chooses the most malicious word of the year in an independent competition titled "Unwort des Jahres," or "Non-word of the Year."
